Leo Qwest

Leo Qwest is a British vocalist and music producer. In March 2015, Leo Qwest released a video for a track called Being Myself.

Infusing Indie Brit pop with Electronic and soul. Experimental and smooth Leo Qwest’s production takes you on a journey to the edge of the universe. Complimented by sharp melodic vocals that open up a door to the world of the dreamer.


Leo Qwest describes his sound as being “music for the dreamers”.


Having experienced performing internationally, Leo Qwest went on to develop a sound that is simply “Leo Qwest”

Currently, Leo Qwest’s distinctive sound is accessible via Soundcloud. In 2015, Leo Qwest launched www.leoqwest.com acting as a hub for fans to keep up with forthcoming shows, music and videos.
